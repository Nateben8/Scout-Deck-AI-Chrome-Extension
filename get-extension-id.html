Step 1: Get Your Extension ID

1. Go to chrome://extensions/

2. Find "ScoutDeck AI" extension

3. Copy the Extension ID (it looks like: abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz123456)

Step 2: Calculate Your Redirect URI

Your redirect URI will be:

https://[YOUR-EXTENSION-ID].chromiumapp.org/extension-auth

For example, if your extension ID is abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz123456, your redirect URI would be:

https://abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz123456.chromiumapp.org/extension-auth

Step 3: Add to Google Cloud Console

1. Go to Google Cloud Console - Credentials

2. Click on your OAuth 2.0 Client ID

3. Under "Authorized redirect URIs", click + ADD URI

4. Add your extension redirect URI:

https://[YOUR-EXTENSION-ID].chromiumapp.org/extension-auth

5. Click Save

Step 4: Test the Extension

1. Reload your extension in chrome://extensions/

2. Click the ScoutDeck AI extension icon

3. Click "Sign in with Google"

4. Complete the OAuth flow - it should now work!
